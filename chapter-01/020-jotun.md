---
title: Fixing Mr. Jötun
---

Lars is sad because his Mr. Jötun is broken.

# How is it broken?
Pick it up and examine it in the inventory.

# How to fix the eye
You need some glue.

# Where do I get glue?
Near the car. In the drawers.

# I have the glue
Combine it with the doll in the inventory.

# It is fixed
Give it back to Lars.
